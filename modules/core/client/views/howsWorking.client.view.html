Secure Payment

TaskMatch Pay is a quick and secure way to get your tasks completed. Once an offer is accepted on a task, the agreed upon amount will be securely held in a TaskMatch Trust Account until the task is completed. When the task is completed, you’ll need to mark the task as completed and the funds will be transferred to their bank account. Make sure you have this set up before you start completing tasks.
